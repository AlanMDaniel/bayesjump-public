Submit your resources here

Help Keep These Resources Free and Growing

Our goal is to create and share high-quality, practical resources for instructors at no cost. Your support helps cover the costs of web hosting, development, and the time dedicated to creating new content.

If you find value in this work, please consider making a contribution to support our community. Every little bit helps ensure this site remains a valuable and accessible tool for educators everywhere.


Other Ways to Show Support

Monetary contributions are deeply appreciated but are not the only way to help! You can also support this project by:

  • Sharing a link to this site with a colleague.
  • Suggesting a resource you’d like to see.
  • Sending a quick email to let me know what you’ve found helpful!
